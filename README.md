# A Spotify Analysis of Taylor Swift's Originals vs. Re-Recordings - Navigating Popularity and Acoustic Evolution.

This project delves into a comprehensive analysis of music streaming data on Spotify, comparing the original versions of well-known songs with their re-recordings. The focus lies on exploring the dynamics of popularity trends and the evolution of acoustic elements, providing insights into how these factors influence listener preferences and contribute to the overall musical landscape.

QUESTIONS TO ANSWER
1. What impact have the re-recordings of her music had on the popularity of the artist's most prominent albums in 2024?
2. Through a comparative analysis of the original albums and their re-recorded counterparts, how has the popularity of the artist's songs demonstrated growth?
3. In correlation with the artist's heightened popularity following the commencement of public re-recordings, is there a discernible enhancement in her acoustics and overall music quality?

HYPOTHESIS
1. All the albums garnering the highest popularity in 2024 are subsequent to the artist's re-recordings.

APPROACH AND ANALYSIS
1. The initial step involved the download of relevant data pertaining to the artist's discography.
2. Subsequent to the download, a data cleansing process was executed to ensure accuracy and reliability.
3. The analysis proceeded by calculating the artist's most popular songs in the year 2024, along with an assessment of her album rankings.
4. Focusing on post re-recordings, an evaluation of the top acoustic performances was conducted.
5. Further analysis involved calculating the popularity of individual songs within each album.
6. The final step comprised a detailed comparison of the extracted data, providing valuable insights into trends and patterns across the artist's musical portfolio.

TECHNICAL CHALLENGES
1. No technical challenges were experienced.

SUMMARY
1. The analysis indicates a notable surge in the artist's popularity since the commencement of re-recording her music, reflecting an expanded reach across a broader audience. While her latest music has demonstrated increased value, it is essential to note that this rise in popularity does not necessarily correlate with an enhancement in acoustic quality in the re-recordings of her original compositions. The inclusion of new unreleased songs within the re-recordings emerges as a significant contributing factor to the observed boost in popularity and heightened recognition.
